What are the most popular colors in YouTube thumbnails?

Taking all the videos on YouTube trending over the last 2 years and getting the color of each pixel in every thumbnail (30,000 thumbnails)
gives us the results below. White is by far the most popular color used in thumbnails. Which makes a lot of sense because if you think about
it each pixel's color is represented by 3 values: Red, Green and Blue. Each color has a number from 0-255. To get awhite pixel you have a
value of 255 - Red, 255 - Green and 255 - Blue; or the maximum value of each color.

Some useful things to note

  1. Colors are bright and radiant
  2. The wide variety of pink and yellow colors are skin colors
  3. 75% of users use dark mode so white constrasts with this well
  4. This graph was created using 31,000 thumbnails from videos on YouTube trending over the last 2 years
